Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 18, 2026  ·  Applies to: constructinv.varshyl.com and the Varshyl Inc. Construction AI Billing platform

Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to run your account and generate your billing documents. We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. Your project data belongs to you and you can delete it any time.

1. Who We Are

Varshyl Inc. ("Varshyl," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Construction AI Billing platform, accessible at constructinv.varshyl.com. We help general contractors, subcontractors, and construction professionals generate AIA G702/G703 pay applications, lien waivers, and billing documents.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at vaakapila@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your plain-text password).
  • Company profile: Company name, contact phone number, contact email, default payment terms, and retainage percentage you enter in Settings.
  • Project data: Project names, addresses, contract amounts, schedule of values, owner and architect information, and pay application figures you enter or upload.
  • Uploaded files: Schedule of Values files (Excel, CSV, PDF, Word), company logos, signature images, and contract documents you upload for processing.
  • Lien documents: Your name, title, and the amount and date you attest to when generating lien waivers or preliminary notices.
  • Feedback: Any feedback, bug reports, or feature requests you submit through the in-app feedback widget, including optional screenshots.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage analytics: We use PostHog to collect anonymized usage events (which pages you visit, which features you use) to understand how to improve the product. PostHog does not receive your project financial data.
  • Authentication logs: IP address and timestamp when you log in, for security purposes.
  • Error logs: Technical error information when something goes wrong, to help us fix bugs.

2.3 Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID from Google. We do not receive your Google password or access to your Google Drive or other Google services.

4. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or project data to any third party.
  • We do not run advertising — not on this platform, not ever. There are no advertisers.
  • We do not use your project data to train AI models.
  • We do not share your financial project data (contract amounts, billing figures) with any external analytics service.

5. Data Sharing and Service Providers

We use a small number of third-party service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Railway — cloud hosting and PostgreSQL database. Your data is stored in Railway's infrastructure. Railway Privacy Policy.
  • Resend / SendGrid — transactional email delivery (account verification, invite emails). Only your email address is shared for this purpose.
  • PostHog — anonymized product analytics. PostHog does not receive identifying information or financial data. PostHog Privacy Policy.
  • Google — if you use Google Sign-In. Google Privacy Policy.

All service providers are contractually required to handle your data only as directed by us and in accordance with applicable privacy law.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Railway's cloud infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • All connections use TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot recover your password.
  • Authentication uses signed JWT tokens with expiration.
  • API endpoints require authentication for all user data access.
  • Rate limiting is applied to all authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • File uploads are validated for type and size before processing.

No method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Project data and documents: Retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual projects at any time.
  • Uploaded files: Retained until you delete the associated project or request deletion.
  • Lien document PDFs: Retained as long as your account is active. These may need to be retained for your records — download copies before deleting your account.
  • Analytics data: Anonymized — not linked to your account after 90 days.
  • Security logs: Retained for 90 days.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update your account information at any time in Settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data. Email us at vaakapila@gmail.com with subject line "Delete my account."
  • Data portability: Request an export of your project data in a standard format.
  • Opt out of analytics: You can opt out of PostHog analytics by using a browser extension that blocks PostHog, or by contacting us.

California residents: You have rights under CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (we don't sell data, so this last right is already protected).

EU/UK residents: You have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR. Our lawful bases are described in Section 3. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following:

  • Authentication token: Stored in your browser's Local Storage to keep you logged in. This is not a cookie — it is not sent to any third party.
  • PostHog analytics cookie: Used to distinguish unique visitors in anonymized analytics. You can block this using browser settings or an ad blocker.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from any advertising network.

10. Children's Privacy

Construction AI Billing is a professional business tool intended for use by adults (18 years or older) in the construction industry. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
